Creation Museum Trip

2018 CCC Youth Winter Educational Trip to Creation Museum

2800 Bullittsburg Church Rd.   Petersburg, KY 41080

*Note: Your registration must be approved by Li-En(Youth Director)

Date: 12/25 (Tue) to 12/29 (Sat)
Driving time estimate: 16 hours

Participants of this trip are required to do one of the following to make the trip more meaningful.
1) Group Presentation (2 people in a group). Your group will present a powerpoint to the Youth describing your experience. Pictures are required in this trip.
2) Writtin Report: Write an essay describing your experiences and what you learned on this trip. Also, include some fun memories of the trip (pictures work really well)!

Personal Expenses

  • $50 Registration Fee

  • $70-$100 for Food

  • More money for Souvenirs (Optional)

  • More money for extra activities (Optional)

More Info

No large suitcases are allowed .
Registration fee and forms are due on 11/4 .
Any disciplinary behavior during the trip will have serious consequences.

Schedule: 
12/24 - (Monday) 9:00 PM - Retrieve rented car (12-seat van)
12/25 - (Tuesday) 5:30 AM - Participants will meet at the church. We will leave CCC at 6:00, we will eat a quick lunch. At night, we will arrive at Embassy Suite, Nashville. There will be three rooms and six people per room.
12/26 - (Wendsday) - Eat breakfast at hotel. In the afternoon, we will explore the Creation Museum. At night, we will stay at the Ashley Quearter Hotel, Florence Kentucky.
12/27 - (Thursday) - We will eat breakfast at the hotel. Then we will visit the Ark Encounter. We will again stay at the same hotel.
12/28 - (Friday) - We will eat breakfast at the hotel, then we will visit the Creation Museum. At night we will go start heading toward Nashville and stay at the Nashville Airport In & Suites.
12/29 - (Saturday) - We will eat breakfast at the hotel, then we will drive back to Houston (late evening)
